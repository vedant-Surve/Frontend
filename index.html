Krushi Mitra

Krushi Mitra (Friend of Farmers) brings digital guidance, government schemes, product listings, weather updates, and expert Q&A into a single farmer-friendly platform.

  • Goal: Digital guidance, schemes, products, weather, and Q&A
  • Vision: Empower farmers, connect experts, reduce middlemen

What you can do

Crop Guidance

Personalized crop suggestions and best practices tailored for your region.

Expert Support

Ask questions and get help from agriculture experts.

Weather & Market

Track weather and prices to plan sowing and selling better.

Schemes & Subsidies

Discover government schemes and benefits that match your profile.

Secure Access
